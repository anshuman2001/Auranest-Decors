Quick Answers

How long does delivery take? +
All orders (India & Worldwide) are delivered in 2–3 business days once confirmed.
What is your return policy? +
We have a hassle-free return & exchange policy. Contact us within 7 days of delivery.
Do you accept bulk/wholesale orders? +
Yes! Email us at info@auranestsdecor.com with your requirements. We have a dedicated wholesale team.
Are there any customs duties? +
No! All customs duties & taxes are borne by Auranest Decors. You only pay the product price + shipping.
What payment options are available? +
India: Cards, UPI, Net Banking, COD. International: Credit Cards, PayPal. All via Razorpay / Stripe.
