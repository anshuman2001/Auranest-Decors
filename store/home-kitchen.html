Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Home & Kitchen collection.

What materials are used in your Home & Kitchen products?
Our Home & Kitchen range is primarily crafted from mango wood, sheesham (rosewood), and reclaimed teak — sourced responsibly from Indian artisan workshops. Surfaces are finished with food-safe lacquers and non-toxic folk art paints, making them safe for table use.
Are the wooden trays and serveware food-safe?
Yes. All our wooden serveware is finished with food-grade, non-toxic coatings. They are suitable for serving dry foods and snacks. We recommend hand-washing only and avoiding prolonged water contact to preserve the natural wood and painted finish.
What folk art styles feature on your kitchen products?
Our kitchen pieces feature Madhubani (Bihar), Warli (Maharashtra), Pichwai (Rajasthan), and Gond (Madhya Pradesh) art styles — each hand-painted by trained artisans. You'll find motifs of peacocks, lotus flowers, village scenes, and geometric patterns that carry centuries of cultural meaning.
How long does delivery take?
All orders are delivered within 2–3 business days across India. Orders above ₹999 qualify for free shipping. For orders below that, a standard shipping fee applies. You'll receive a tracking link as soon as your order is dispatched.
Can I return or exchange kitchen products?
Yes, we offer a hassle-free return policy within 7 days of delivery, provided the product is unused and in its original packaging. Since each piece is handcrafted, minor natural variations in colour and grain are not considered defects but part of the artisan character of the product.
Are these products suitable as gifts?
Absolutely! Our handcrafted kitchen pieces make beautiful gifts for housewarmings, weddings, Diwali, and festivals. Each product comes carefully packaged, and we offer gift wrapping on request. Browse our Giftings collection for curated gift ideas.
