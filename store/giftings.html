Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about gifting handcrafted Indian artisan pieces.

Can I get gift wrapping for my order?
Yes! Gift wrapping is available on request at no extra charge. Simply mention in the order notes that you'd like gift packaging, and whether you'd like a handwritten gift message included. We use premium recycled paper wrapping with gold ribbon — befitting a truly artisan gift.
What are good handcrafted gifts for Diwali?
Our most popular Diwali gifts include handpainted wooden trays (perfect for serving mithai), Jharokha wall pieces, folk art decorative pots, and our curated artisan gift sets. For a complete guide, read our blog: 10 Thoughtful Handcrafted Gifts for Diwali 2026.
Do you offer bulk gifting for corporate orders?
Yes, we offer corporate gifting solutions for bulk orders of 10 pieces or more. We can customise packaging with your company logo, create bespoke gift hampers, and arrange bulk delivery. Contact us at info@auranestsdecor.com with your requirements and we'll create a proposal within 24 hours.
Can I send a gift directly to the recipient?
Absolutely. Simply enter the recipient's address as the delivery address during checkout. If you'd like to include a personalised message, add it in the order notes. We ship across India in 2–3 business days. For time-sensitive deliveries, please contact us to check express options.
What makes handcrafted gifts better than store-bought ones?
Handcrafted gifts carry a story — of the artisan who made them, the region they come from, and the tradition they represent. They are unique (no two are exactly alike), built to last, and carry genuine cultural value. In a world of mass production, a handcrafted gift says you chose something truly special.
What is the return policy for gifts?
We offer hassle-free returns within 7 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. If the gift arrives damaged, we'll replace it immediately at no charge — just send us a photo within 48 hours of delivery. Since items are handcrafted, natural variations in colour and finish are not considered defects.
