Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Aesthetic Furniture collection.

What types of wood are used in your furniture pieces?
Our furniture is crafted primarily from mango wood, sheesham (Indian rosewood), and reclaimed wood — selected for their strength, natural grain, and workability. Mango wood has a warm honey tone; sheesham is darker with striking grain patterns. Both are sustainably sourced from Indian artisan workshops.
How are these furniture pieces different from mass-produced ones?
Every piece in our Aesthetic Furniture collection is hand-crafted by individual artisans — no factory assembly lines. The joinery, carving, and finishing are all done by hand, which means minor natural variations in grain, colour, and texture. These variations are not defects — they are the hallmarks of genuine handcraft and make each piece unique.
Are the shelves and racks sturdy enough for regular use?
Yes. Our shelves and racks are built using traditional joinery techniques — mortise and tenon, dowel joints — that are actually stronger than machine-produced furniture. They are designed to hold books, plants, decorative objects, and similar items. Each product listing includes the recommended weight capacity.
Do the furniture pieces come with assembly instructions?
Smaller pieces like racks and decorative shelves are usually delivered fully assembled. Larger shelf units may come partially assembled with simple instructions included. All required hardware (screws, anchors, brackets) is included in the packaging. For any assembly queries, our support team is available at info@auranestsdecor.com.
How do I care for the wooden furniture?
Wipe with a soft, dry cloth for routine cleaning. Avoid placing in direct sunlight for extended periods or near damp areas. Apply furniture wax or teak oil every 6–12 months to nourish the wood and maintain its lustre. Avoid harsh chemical cleaners, abrasive scrubbers, or steam cleaning on natural wood surfaces.
Can I get custom dimensions for furniture pieces?
Yes, we offer customisation for bulk or special orders (3 pieces or more). You can specify dimensions within structural limits, wood type, finish, and colour. Lead time for custom pieces is typically 10–15 business days. Write to us at info@auranestsdecor.com with your requirements.
