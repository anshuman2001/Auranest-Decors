We didn't start Auranest Decors to sell products.

We started it to preserve something rare — the art of making things by hand, with skill, with patience, and with pride. 🤍

India has thousands of artisans in Rajasthan, Bihar, Maharashtra and Gujarat who paint Madhubani, carve Jharokhas, throw pottery, and shape terracotta with techniques passed down for generations.

We work directly with them. Every piece in our collection is made by skilled craftspeople — no shortcuts, no machines, no mass production.

When you bring an Auranest Decors piece home, you're not just decorating a space. You're keeping an art form alive. ✨
